Explanation & Details
Crit Scoring: The total percentage value of rolled crit rate/damage out of a possible 200% per echo. Example: Rolling 14 out of the possible 21 crit damage (without any crit rate) would result in a crit scoring of ~66.66%. Roll Scoring: The total percentage value of all stats (without character-specific weighting applied) in comparison to the maximum possible value for the given stat. A high roll score doesn't equal a well-equipped character due to character-specific stats not being applied, causing for example a high DEF% roll on an ATK-scaling character to have the same value as ATK%. Perfection: The total percentage value of all stats weighted by the listed character-specific modifiers. This rating is displayed in a letter-based format for easier understanding. While character-specific weighting is applied, secondary requirements like energy regen and crit ratios are not checked and can result in performing below expectations. Always use this stat with your current crit scoring and character-specific secondary requirements in mind.
